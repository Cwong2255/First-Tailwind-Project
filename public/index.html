Locations

New York

New York City comprises of five boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. At its core is Manhattan, a densely populated borough that's among the world's major commercial, financial and cultural centers. Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building and sprawling Central Park.

San Francisco

Famous for scenic beauty, cultural attractions, diverse communities and world-class cuisine, San Francisco's landmarks include the Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars, Fisherman's Wharf, Alcatraz, Chinatown, Union Square, North Beach, the Castro district and Mission Dolores. The much-celebrated fog creates a romantic mood in this most European of American cities.

Chicago

When you're in Chicago, you're home. Discover big-city culture, Midwestern hospitality, and urban adventure, from iconic skyscrapers and celebrated museums to miles of sandy beaches.
